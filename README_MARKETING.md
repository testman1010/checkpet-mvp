# CheckPet: Product & Marketing Brief

**For:** Chief Marketing Officer / Growth Team
**Version:** MVP 1.0 (Active Monitoring Release)

---

## 🚀 The Elevator Pitch
**CheckPet is the "On-Star" for Pet Health.**
We are not just another symptom checker. CheckPet is an active guardian that triages your pet’s emergency instantly and *stays with you* to monitor their recovery. It combines veterinary-grade AI precision with the comfort of a proactive SMS check-in, bridging the terrifying gap between "Googling symptoms" and "Paying $200 at the ER."

---

## 💎 Core Value Propositions (The "Why")

### 1. Veterinary-Grade Peace of Mind (Trust)
*   **Feature:** RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology that references trusted veterinary manuals (Merck Vet Manual).
*   **Marketing Angle:** "Stop doom-scrolling. Get answers grounded in real veterinary science, not forums."
*   **User Benefit:** eliminates the panic of misinformation.

### 2. We Don't Just Diagnose, We Monitor (Retention)
*   **Feature:** Automated SMS Check-In (Active Monitoring).
*   **Marketing Angle:** "We check in so you don't have to worry alone."
*   **User Benefit:** Two hours after triage, we text the user to ask if the pet is better or worse. If worse, we escalate. This turns a transactional visit into a *relationship*.
*   **Differentiation:** Competitors give an answer and leave. We stay.

### 3. Frictionless Access (Top of Funnel)
*   **Feature:** 100% Free, Anonymous, No Sign-Up Required.
*   **Marketing Angle:** "Help in seconds, not forms."
*   **User Benefit:** In an emergency, no one wants to create an account. We remove 100% of the friction to get help.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience Personas

### "The Anxious New Parent"
*   **Who:** Millennials/Gen-Z who adopted a 'pandemic puppy' or kitten.
*   **Pain Point:** Every sneeze looks like an emergency. They have high disposable income for their pet but low medical knowledge.
*   **Campaign Hook:** "Is it an emergency or just a hairball? Know for sure in 30 seconds."

### "The Cost-Conscious Guardian"
*   **Who:** Families who love their pets but fear the unexpected $500 vet bill.
*   **Pain Point:** Financial anxiety. They dread the "better safe than sorry" trip to the ER that turns out to be nothing.
*   **Campaign Hook:** "Save the ER trip for real emergencies. CheckPet is free."

---

## ⚡ The User Journey (Marketing Narrative)

1.  **Trigger:** It's 11 PM. The dog eats a sock. Panic sets in.
2.  **Discovery:** User searches "Dog ate sock urgency" -> Finds CheckPet.
3.  **The Relief (Triage):** They type "My dog ate a sock." CheckPet instantly assesses urgency ("Consult Vet" vs "Emergency").
4.  **The Hook (Active Monitoring):** Results page says: *"Monitor this closely. Enter your number, and we'll check back in 2 hours to see if he vomits."*
5.  **The Relationship:** Phone buzzes 2 hours later. *"Hi, it's CheckPet. Is Cooper still acting normal?"* User replies *"Yes"*. CheckPet says *"Great. Monitor for X, Y, Z."*
6.  **Loyalty:** User feels supported and safe. Next time, they come straight to CheckPet.

## 🛡 Legal & Brand Safety (CRITICAL)

### **"Information Only" Mandate**
*   **Disclaimer:** CheckPet is an information tool, **not a veterinary device**. We do not provide medical diagnoses or prescriptions.
*   **User Clarity:** Every interaction explicitly states: *"This is AI-generated information. If you suspect an emergency, go to a vet immediately."*
*   **Safety First Logic:** Our algorithms are tuned for "False Positives" (better safe than sorry). If there is *any* ambiguity, the system recommends a vet visit. We never discourage professional medical care.

### **Privacy & Data Ethics**
*   **Zero-Sale Policy:** Phone numbers are strictly for the active monitoring session. We do not sell user data to third parties.
*   **Anonymity:** No account required means no PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is stored beyond the necessary session data.
